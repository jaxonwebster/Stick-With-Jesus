Who We Are

Spreading Light in Simple Spaces

Stick With Jesus was born out of a desire to surround ourselves with simple, daily encouragements. In the hustle and noise of everyday life, having a small reminder of God’s promise on your water bottle, laptop, or mirror can turn a fleeting moment into a prayerful pause.

Every sticker we create starts as a prayer and a hand-drawn sketch—crafted carefully to inspire hope, start sweet conversations, and shine His light wherever you go.
